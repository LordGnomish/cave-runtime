## Becoming a Maintainer

Consistent, high-quality contributions over time. Maintainers are added by consensus of existing maintainers.

## Decision Making

- Minor changes: single maintainer approval
- Major changes: documented ADR + comment period
- Breaking changes: 14-day comment window required
